[00:01:09] Participant: The messy part was the handoff. I don't need a twenty-page report. I need the three things that changed the roadmap and the exact quotes that back them up.

[00:02:20] Participant: Notes were in one place, recordings were somewhere else, and the final decision lived in a planning doc. Two weeks later nobody could find the original evidence.
[00:03:31] Interviewer: How would AI help in that workflow?
